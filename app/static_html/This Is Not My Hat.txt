			  WINNER OF THE 2014 KATE GREENAWAY MEDAL<br><br>Klassen&rsquo;s authorial debut, I WANT MY HAT BACK (2011), became one of the surprise picture-book hits of the year, and while it&rsquo;s tempting to see this follow-up as a sequel, it&rsquo;s really only related in its hat-theft theme, animal characters, deadpan humor, and a suggestively dark conclusion. . . .
