    			<p><b>The Caldecott Honor winning classic about a brave and clever little worm from beloved picture book creator Leo Lionni.</b><br><br>A small green inchworm is proud of his skill at measuring anything&mdash;a robin&rsquo;s tail, a flamingo&rsquo;s neck, a toucan&rsquo;s beak. Then one day a nightingale threatens to eat him if he cannot measure his song. The bold graphics and beautiful cutout paper collages of colorful birds, foliage, and the clever green inch worm are as fresh and appealing as ever, and should appeal to a whole new audience in this accessible board book format.</p>	 	</section>
